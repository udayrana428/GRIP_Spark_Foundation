About Us

Jeevan NGO, a non-profit charity organization, proudly operates over 480 branches across India, touching every facet of life with its comprehensive approach. Beginning by addressing the root causes of disability, Jeevan NGO provides corrective surgeries and extends support by offering free education and meals to the underprivileged.

The commitment of Jeevan NGO extends to empowering individuals with visual, hearing, and speech impairments through life skills training and specialized education. Moreover, professional skill development programs are provided for people with disabilities. Established in 1985 under the name A Fistful of Flour, where meals were served to the physically and economically disadvantaged, the mission of Jeevan NGO has evolved significantly. Presently, Jeevan NGO offers free corrective surgeries to patients suffering from polio and congenital disabilities, along with providing free artificial limbs to amputees.

Headquartered in Udaipur, Rajasthan, India, Jeevan NGO's hospital, boasting a collective capacity of 1100 beds, attracts patients from across India and the world for polio-related treatments and corrective surgeries. Regardless of caste, creed, or religion, Jeevan NGO has conducted over 4,39,459 free-of-cost polio corrective surgeries to date, continuing to transform lives.
